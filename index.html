About Me

My name is Emma Weber and I am an aspiring Web Developer. I am currently in a 12 week full stack, full-time coding boot camp at the University of Denver. After a few years working for a start up in downtown Denver I've decided to change career paths and challenge myself to try something new! My interest in coding was sparked by a project I worked on with the Eng team in my most recent position--the colors, the layering, the ability to build something with creativity and skill, all caught my attention.

So here we are, a look into my portfolio. A WIP to say the least, but I'm proud of what I've learned so far and am excited to keep progressing!
